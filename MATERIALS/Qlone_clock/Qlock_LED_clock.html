Qlone LED clock



The Qlone (Qlocktwo-Clone) is my version of the Qlocktwo Clock by Biegert & Funk. 

 It is comprised of:
   - 1 Arduino-clone (the Boarduino)
   - 114 white LEDs
   - 2 MAX 7219 LED drivers
   - 1 Chronodot real-time clock
   - 1 photoresistor

The photoresistor measures the relative ambient light in the room.  From that, the LED brightness is adjusted accordingly.

The clock features three different time modes, a test mode, and an Anniversary Mode.  See the video for each mode in action.

The Arduino code is available here.

Special thanks to Flickr-users Ruud Burger and mdotliang for their photos,  explanations of their build-ups, and their code (which I only had to adapt slightly for my needs).  I certainly wouldn't have been able to do it without their help.
