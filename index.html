Who Am I?


When I was 11, I read THE WAY THINGS WORK cover-to-cover...and I haven't stopped geeking out about how things work since.


Imagining something new and then doing whatever I need to bring it to life is what gets me out of bed in the morning. I'll learn whatever it takes, stretch my comfort zone, and work tirelessly to make it real.

I have built an extensive set of skills from my time in commercial aerospace, robotics, drones and a product design consultancy; from mechanisms to mechatronics, structures to software, prototypes to technical presentations. Currently, I am solving tough technology problems for our clients at TEAGUE.

When I'm not making things, I can be found riding bikes, drinking coffee, soaking up the Pacific Northwest outdoors, and trying (generally unsuccessfully) to keep up with my kids.

Thanks for stopping by!
