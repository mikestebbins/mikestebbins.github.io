Reardar: Radar for Cyclists

At work, we wondered if an inexpensive Doppler radar module (~$3 on ebay) could be used as a sort of "cyclist radar" to sense what was coming up behind you. In a quick sprint over a couple of slow days, a colleague of mine assembled an amplifier & microcontroller circuit to read the radar while I took care of digital signal processing on the noisy data and conducting simple tests to see what real-world performance might look like.
