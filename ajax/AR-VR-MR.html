Experimenting with Augmented / Virtual / Mixed Reality

In 2013, like so many others, I became enamored with the Google Cardboard and all of the possibilities that it created for VR. I was interested in using it as a tool in engineering and design as a means to quickly experience concepts without building physical things and to display data too complex for 2-D.

In my free time, I began building 3D virtual reality scenes in Unity for the Cardboard and also started experimenting with augmented reality using Processing and a webcam. We are increasingly using tools like these for client work, but I can't show anything off (not yet, at least).
