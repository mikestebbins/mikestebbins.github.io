Espresso Machine PID Temperature Controller

A precise, accurate espresso machine temperature is an essential part of the journey towards espresso enlightenment, also known as "the God-Shot". To that end, I replaced the wildly-imprecise thermostat in my machine with a thermocouple and a PID (Proportional Integral Derivative) temperature controller.

This was my first electronics project many years ago, as you can maybe tell by the fire extinguisher in the frame, but I didn't burn down our house and it still pulls "God Shots" frequently!
