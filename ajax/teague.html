Mechanical Designer at TEAGUE

At TEAGUE, the Original Design Consultancy, I work with world-class talent to bring innovative products and experiences to life in the travel and technology spaces. Thinking Through Making is part of the company's DNA, a huge part of how we succeed (and fail) quickly, and the perfect work environment for someone who loves to make things; I'm able to "get my hands dirty" building as I design, and I wouldn't have it any other way!
