Overhead Timelapse Rig

My friend Jeff Skierka (designer behind the Mixtape Table) wanted to film timelapse videos of projects being built on his work-bench from overhead, while circling around the bench. I thought about it for a bit, came up with the stepper motor/bike wheel drive idea, and told him to start building that. Meanwhile, I threw together the circuitry for an adjustable-speed, stepper-motor-drive system with a homing feature and a pause switch (to allow for multi-day, multi-start films ) and a DIY intervalometer to capture photos from his SLR. We were happy with the results, given the short amount of time put into it.
